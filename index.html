F.A.Q.

Frequently Asked Questions

We achieved to make this box free for the enduser due to the high costs savings for all logistic companies. They are paying us monthly for providing all Boxtures. So just enjoy!

The box will be delivered directly to you by one of our craftman partners. If wished, they also fix the box into the ground for an even higher security level.

Boxtures have a standard size of 17.5 x 17.5 x 22.5 Zoll. This guarantees the delivery of all small to medium packages.

Every Boxture has a unique QR-Code which secures the Boxture. Through scanning the code via our Smartphone App you can unlock the Boxture anytime. Postmen can only open your Boxture if they have a delivery for you or if you scheduled a return.

Boxtures consist of thick aluminium and are anchored in the ground. Therefore, neither breaking them apart nor carrying away is possible in a reasonably time.

Firstly, you just schedule your return as per usual and indicate that you would like the logistic provider to collect it from your home.
Secondly, you put the prepared package into you Boxture.
Thirdly, enjoy and do not worry anymore!
